## Project Overview
**marbles** is a marble roller game built with Google Filament and Rapier.js physics.

- **Core Fun**: Physics-based marble rolling with satisfying movement and interaction.
- **Tech Focus**: High-quality 3D rendering + realistic physics in the browser.

## Technology Stack
- JavaScript + Filament (Google’s 3D engine) + Rapier.js

## Grok Guidelines
- **Physics Feel**: The joy comes from how the marbles roll, bounce, and interact — prioritize realistic and fun physics.
- **Visual Quality**: Filament can deliver great visuals — lean into good lighting, materials, and camera work.
- **Level Design**: Interesting tracks, obstacles, and goals will make it more engaging.
- **Performance**: Keep smooth framerates even with multiple marbles and complex scenes.
